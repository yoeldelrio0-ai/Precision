Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner to Concrete & Stone Installation

Precision Concrete & Masonry has been a cornerstone across North and South Carolina. We specialize in brick, block, stone, and concrete work for residential and commercial clients. Every project is handled with precision, pride, and the hands-on experience it takes to do this work right.


From driveways and retaining walls to full structural masonry, our craftsmen bring skill and care to every build. We take on projects of all sizes — and we treat every client like our only client.

Concrete Work

Precision Concrete

Concrete done right is the foundation of everything we build. We pour, finish, and cure every slab, driveway, and flatwork with the precision that our name demands — no cracks, no shortcuts, no settling for second-best.


Whether you need a new driveway, a reinforced foundation, or a stamped concrete patio, our concrete specialists deliver results you'll be proud of for decades to come.

Expert Masonry

Brick and block masonry done with true craftsmanship stands apart. We lay every course level, plumb, and perfectly jointed — built to weather the elements and look great doing it.


From structural block foundations to decorative brick facades, retaining walls, steps, and outdoor living spaces — our masons bring the skill and care every project deserves.
