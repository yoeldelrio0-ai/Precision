Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner to Concrete & Stone Installation

Precision Concrete & Masonry has been a cornerstone of southeastern North Carolina for years. We specialize in brick, block, stone, and concrete work for residential and commercial clients. Every project is handled with precision, pride, and the hands-on experience it takes to do this work right.


From driveways and retaining walls to full structural masonry, our craftsmen bring skill and care to every build. We take on projects of all sizes — and we treat every client like our only client.

Precision Concrete

Concrete done right is the foundation of everything we build. We pour, finish, and cure every slab, driveway, and flatwork with the precision that our name demands — no cracks, no shortcuts, no settling for second-best.
